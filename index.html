Program Highlights

An unforgettable blend of academics, culture, and adventure in one of Europe's most vibrant cities

6 Credit Hours

Take ECON 4085 and ECON 4360, covering Spanish/EU economics, history, culture, and business. Counts as upper-level credits for ECON or COBA majors.

World-Class Speakers

Learn from exceptional professors and industry speakers at Universitat Abat Oliba CEU. All classes and visits conducted in English.

Prime Location

Stay near Passeig de Grcia with beaches minutes away. Unlimited metro access includedexplore Barcelona freely!

Stress-Free Learning

No homework or exams while in Spainfocus on the experience. Exams and essays completed online after returning home.

What You'll Experience

From world-famous landmarks to unique business visits and meaningful service opportunities

Business & Social Enterprise Visits

L'Olivera Winery

Last year's student favorite! Visit a stunning Mediterranean winery that trains at-risk youth for careers in hospitality. Walk among grape vines and olive trees, then enjoy wine and olive oil tastings in one of the most beautiful landscapes you'll ever see.

Fundaci Trinijove

A famous European foundation helping at-risk youth and migrants integrate into Spanish society. Includes an amazing tour of Barcelona's underground water system.

Apunts Social Enterprise

Learn about an innovative business model where 75% of employees have certified mental health conditions, working in printing and advertising.

Delta del Ebro & Buda Island

Official reception by the Mayor of Sant Jaume d'Enveja, wetland exploration, and visit to a natural island with wildlife and a horse sanctuary. Possible overnight stay!

Cultural Tours (All with English-Speaking Guides)

La Sagrada Familia

Experience Gaud's masterpiece in personone of the most iconic and awe-inspiring buildings in the world.

Picasso Museum

Explore the life and work of the greatest artist of the 20th century, who lived in Barcelona and was born nearby.

The Gothic Quarter & Barcelona Cathedral

Wander through one of Europe's best-preserved medieval neighborhoodsnarrow streets, historic plazas, small bars, and hidden gems.

King's Place & Roman Barcelona

Dr. Gonzalez's personal favorite! An incredible museum revealing Barcelona's Roman history right in the heart of the Gothic Quarter.

Refuge 307

Second student favorite! Enter a real bomb shelter from the Spanish Civil War and learn about its connection to World War II. Fascinating and slightly eerie.

Roman Funeral Way

A fascinating look at Roman burial practices in Barcelona over 2,400 years ago.

Service & Community

Sant Ana Church

Volunteer to feed the homeless in the Gothic Quarteran extremely touching experience for many of us.

Barcelona Food Bank

Help people in need while learning about community support systems in Spain.

Free Time & Social Life

Beaches, Nightlife & Exploration

Afternoons and weekends are yours! Barcelona's beaches are minutes away. Beautiful Mediterranean beaches with crystal-clear water are just 30 minutes out. Social events with Spanish students at our host university. Incredible food, nightlife, and cultural experiences for all budgets.
